What is Miguel Outdoors?

Miguel Outdoors is the primary home for documenting all that I love to do. I write about my passions, interesting places that I’ve been, and I interview amazing people that I’ve met throughout my life. The goal is to develop a long journal of music, art, movement, and a love for the outdoors that I can both remember for myself, Â and share with others. I’m inspired by people like: Ben Kielesinski, Elina Osborne , and so many people in my local community.

The Normal Travel Blog

Part of this site will be a normal travel blog. I’ll go to places, share logistic tips, take fun pictures, and write about my experience.

Artist Interviews

Another part will hopefully soon be a list of interviews on amazing people that I meet along the way. From aerial artists, to full time travelers, to local photographers and so on.

Travel Guidance

This last part is still under development, if at any point I can provide a guide service, whether that be for surfing, kayaking, or certain trails in Virgina, my goal is to be able to physically take people out to enjoy the adventures I’ve come to love. But there’s a lot of certifications, safety, and other matter that I need to flesh out first.
