Winter Update 2023

Wednesday, Mar 8, 2023

I don’t know what to write here anymore. There are so many things going on in life and it feels like we’ll never have the time to fully process it all. Timelines, projects, shows, and everything else that life has to throw at you. Lots of things that could be considered good and lot of things that could be considered bad. I am overwhelmed, and this is how I’m feeling, and that’s okay.

On the bright side, I’ve been enjoying so many fun new things. To include:

  • Paintings
  • Painted Cigarette Box
  • Fancy water vessels.
  • Cord
  • Cards
  • Chalking my walls
  • Kintsugi
  • Drawing

I think I will be retiring this site for now. I want to save some money. I will try to maybe do a static site on github. We’ll see where that goes. It fits what I do more and I’m more used to writing in this way. Hopefully then more writing will happen. I forgot why I was even writing in the first place. Maybe I never knew. But I think I will keep going.
