What is Miguel Outdoors?

Miguel Outdoors is the primary home for documenting all that I love to do. I write about my passions, interesting places that I’ve been, and I interview amazing people that I’ve met throughout my life. The goal is to develop a long journal of music, art, movement, and a love for the outdoors that I can both remember for myself, and share with others. I’m inspired by people like: Ben Kielesinski, Elina Osborne, as well as all the amazing people I’ve met here in Hampton Roads, Virginia.

Welcome! Stay awhile if you like. I invite you to check out the newsletter. And, if you feel so inclined there’s always the option to buy me a coffee. ☕ Thank you very much for stopping by!
